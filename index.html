Daily Campus Life

A typical day in my college life is full of learning and fun experiences. Starting college as a first-year student was a bit overwhelming because the environment is very different from high school. However, it has been enjoyable as I get to try new things every day. I have to wake up early to commute to school, which is something I didn’t have to do in high school. Every day is fulfilling because I’ve made close friends and classmates who support me in our activities. Spending time with them is fun, and I truly enjoy learning and growing together.
